Study of Sentences

An exploration of sentences as complete operators acting on invariant structures. This repository collects notes, definitions, operators, and worked examples.

Overview Sections Manuscript GitHub Repo

Overview

This project treats every complete sentence as carrying a full operator–invariant structure. The repository is organized into conceptual components: invariants, operators, and concrete sentence studies, plus supporting notes.

Sections in this project

  • Invariants
    Definitions and notes on the underlying invariant structures.
  • Operators
    Operator notions that act on or relate invariants, aligned with sentence structure.
  • Sentence Studies
    Concrete analyses of individual sentences as expressions of the operator–invariant system.
  • Docs
    Additional documentation, background material, and supporting notes.
